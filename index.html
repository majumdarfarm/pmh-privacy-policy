Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Majumdar Farm ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Poultry Medicine & Hatching mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, disclosed, and protected when you use our App.

1. About the App

Poultry Medicine & Hatching is an educational mobile application designed to provide poultry healthcare, medicine, disease management, incubation, and hatching guidance for poultry farmers, students, farm workers, and poultry enthusiasts.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not require user registration and does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Postal address
  • Phone number
  • Government identification numbers
  • Payment information

However, certain information may be collected automatically by third-party services integrated into the App.

3. Local Device Storage

To improve user experience, the App stores certain information locally on your device using Android DataStore.

This may include:

  • Selected language preference
  • Bookmarked articles
  • Reading progress information
  • Notification preferences
  • Onboarding completion status

This information remains stored on your device and is not transmitted to Majumdar Farm servers.

4. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect and process certain information, including:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • IP address (processed by Google)
  • Diagnostic information
  • Ad interaction information

This information is processed by Google in accordance with its own privacy practices.

For more information, please review:

Google Privacy Policy

5. User Consent for Advertising

Where required by applicable laws and regulations, the App uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage user consent for personalized advertising.

Users may manage advertising preferences through available consent options presented within the App.

6. Push Notifications

The App uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver notifications.

Notifications may include:

  • Educational content updates
  • New article announcements
  • Important application updates
  • General poultry farming information

You may disable notifications through your device settings or the App settings, where available.

7. Third-Party Services

The App currently uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the App.

The App follows Android security best practices, including:

  • Restricted WebView configuration
  • Disabled JavaScript for local article content
  • Secure local data storage
  • Limited permission usage

While we strive to protect user information, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

10. International Users

Third-party service providers such as Google may process information on servers located in various countries.

By using the App, you acknowledge that information may be processed in accordance with applicable laws and the policies of those service providers.
